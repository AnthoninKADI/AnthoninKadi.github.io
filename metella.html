La Vengeance de Metella

Unreal Engine
3D
Narrative
Stealth
Theatre Adaptation
Partnership Project

In La Vengeance de Metella, the player takes control of Metella inside the Quimper-Karadec hotel. Jealous of Raoul de Gardefeu’s new romantic conquest, she decides to sabotage his seduction plan involving the Baroness of Gondremarck.

To achieve her goal, Metella must deliver an incriminating letter to the Baroness while avoiding Gardefeu’s companions, who will attempt to stop her throughout the hotel.

Developed during the 3rd year at ARTFX over one month, the project focused on adapting the famous theatrical piece La Vengeance de Metella into an immersive 3D experience in partnership with the Opéra Orchestre de Montpellier.

Development Challenges

  • Adapting a theatrical work into interactive 3D gameplay.
  • Designing stealth and narrative-driven mechanics inside a complex environment.
  • Creating believable atmosphere and character interactions inspired by stage performances.
  • Collaborating with artistic and cultural partners during production.

What you'll experience

  • Explore the luxurious Quimper-Karadec hotel.
  • Evade guards and opponents while progressing through the story.
  • Experience a theatrical narrative adapted into a modern interactive format.
  • Discover a student production blending stage inspiration with gameplay.
