A Light Seeker

Unity
2.5D
Team Project
Exploration

A Light Seeker is the second large team project of 1.5 months created on Unity in 2.5D.

The player follows a mysterious scream and encounters strange objects and unusual locations, uncovering a new clue at each level that drives the story forward. The adventure combines exploration and puzzles, creating an immersive and captivating experience that draws the player into its mysterious world.

A Light Seeker immerses the player in a dark, mysterious world where every shadow and beam of light contributes to the suspense and storytelling.

Challenges of the project

  • Developing immersive lighting and shadow mechanics to enhance the dark atmosphere.
  • Creating interactive puzzles and objects with subtle visual feedback.
  • My contribution: I worked on the monster, the outline shader, the checkpoint system, and the mechanics for shooting and pushing objects.

What you’ll experience

  • Follow a mysterious scream across dark and eerie levels.
  • Explore unusual objects and locations filled with strange particles.
  • Solve puzzles and uncover clues that reveal the story step by step.
