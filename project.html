Sharkour!

📅 January 30th 2026 → February 1st 2026

Sharkour! is a 3D game developed with Unreal Engine 5 during the 2026 Global Game Jam. It centers on a unique mask-switching mechanic that drives both exploration and traversal. Players use different masks, each linked to a specific color, to reveal hidden platforms and navigate the environment. A special mask, limited by duration and cooldown, allows players to see all platforms at once, adding a layer of timing and strategy. To intensify the experience, a relentless wall of water filled with sharks constantly chases the player, creating continuous pressure and urgency.

Another World Remake

📅 2024

Another World Remake is a 3D game developed with Unreal Engine as a first-year group project. It recreates the prison section of Eric Chahi’s classic Another World in 3D. Players, trapped by an alien civilization, must escape with another prisoner using a weapon that fires lasers and creates energy shields. The game blends third-person action and puzzle-solving while staying true to the original experience.

La Vengeance de Metella

📅 2023

La Vengeance de Metella is a 2.5D game developed with Unreal Engine as a group project in collaboration with the Orchestre National de Montpellier. Players take on the role of Metella, who, jealous of Raoul de Gardefeu’s new conquest, must deliver an incriminating letter to the Baroness of Gondremarck while avoiding Gardefeu’s companions. The game blends stealth, strategy, and narrative-driven gameplay in a unique historical setting.
