Sand Wars

Unity
2D
Shooter
Arcade

Sand Wars is a 2D side-scrolling shooter project developed in Unity. The game focuses on fast-paced survival gameplay where the player must eliminate incoming waves of enemies while maintaining control of a constantly escalating battlefield.

The core experience revolves around reaction time, positioning, and consistent shooting mechanics, creating a simple but highly replayable arcade-style loop designed for short and intense play sessions.

This project helped reinforce gameplay programming fundamentals such as enemy spawning systems, difficulty scaling, and responsive player controls.

Features

  • Fast-paced 2D side-scrolling shooting gameplay.
  • Multiple enemy types with distinct behaviors.
  • Endless survival-based scoring system.
  • Arcade-style replayability focused on skill improvement.
  • My contribution: I worked on enemy AI, spawning system, player shooting mechanics, and gameplay balancing.

What you’ll experience

  • Constant action with waves of enemies attacking the player.
  • Simple controls focused on movement and shooting efficiency.
  • Increasing difficulty over time to test survival skills.
  • Arcade-inspired gameplay designed for replayability.

Sand Wars represents a focused arcade prototype emphasizing gameplay clarity, responsiveness, and escalation over time.
